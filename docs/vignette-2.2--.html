Chapter 4 Vignette 2.2: —-

4.1 Ok… what happens if we cannot randomize?

df <- data.frame(income = runif(10000)) %>% # In this case, your health is determined randomly AND by your levels of income mutate(health_no_pill = rnorm(10000) + income, health_w_pill = health_no_pill + 1) %>% # Now we give the pill only to people that have money mutate(pill = income > .7, health_obs = ifelse(pill==1,health_w_pill,health_no_pill))

4.2 Let’s see what happens now…

df %>% group_by(pill) %>% summarize(health = mean(health_obs))

4.3 But when properly modeled (this is an RDD!)…

df %>% filter(abs(income-.7)<.01) %>% group_by(pill) %>% summarize(health = mean(health_obs)) ## BOOM!!
